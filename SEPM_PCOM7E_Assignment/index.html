External relevance and impact

The themes explored within this module, particularly digital governance, risk management, and software dependency, have extended beyond the academic context. Insights derived from research conducted during the MSc programme contributed to a policy briefing for members of the Dutch House of Representatives (Tweede Kamer) on digital sovereignty and cloud dependency.

This experience demonstrates the applicability of SEPM concepts in real-world decision-making environments, linking academic analysis to public-sector software engineering and governance challenges.
