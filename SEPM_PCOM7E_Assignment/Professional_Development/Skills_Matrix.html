Professional Skills Matrix

Module: Software Engineering Project Management (SEPM_PCOM7E)

This skills matrix reflects the professional competencies developed and demonstrated during the SEPM_PCOM7E module. Each skill is evaluated in relation to module activities, teamwork, and applied project management practice.

Skill Evidence from Module Current Level Reflection
Time Management Unit planning, assignment deadlines, team coordination Advanced Experience in managing parallel academic and professional responsibilities enabled effective planning and prioritisation.
Critical Thinking & Analysis Risk analysis (Unit 3), quality evaluation (Unit 9, 10) Advanced Applied analytical frameworks to evaluate uncertainty, technical debt, and software quality.
Problem Solving Behave BDD project, traceability, quality improvements Advanced Demonstrated structured problem-solving in both technical and managerial contexts.
Teamwork & Collaboration Collaborative discussions, team meetings, group artefacts Advanced Worked effectively within a distributed team, contributing constructively to shared outcomes.
Leadership Deliberate decision to limit leadership responsibilities during the module Developing I intentionally did not take on a leadership role in this module due to limited capacity. This affected my level of contribution and, in hindsight, likely contributed to a lower outcome (6.4 compared to my usual average of 7.4). The key learning is to proactively negotiate a realistic role early (e.g., smaller but clearly owned responsibilities) and communicate constraints transparently to protect both team delivery and individual performance.
Communication & Literacy Wiki posts, discussions, reports, presentations Advanced Produced clear, structured, and academically appropriate written communication.
Digital & IT Skills Code artefacts, tooling, automation, documentation Advanced Applied professional tooling and practices aligned with industry standards.
Emotional Intelligence Team interaction, conflict awareness, reflective practice Proficient Maintained awareness of group dynamics and adapted communication style accordingly.
